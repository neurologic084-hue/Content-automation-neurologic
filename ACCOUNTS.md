# Accounts the video app uses

All of these are signed into with **Google** — use "Continue with Google" on
each site.

---

## Paid

| Service | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Railway** | Runs the app itself |
| **Submagic** | Makes versions 1, 2 and 3 of every video |
| **OpenRouter** | The AI — writes the scripts, decides the cuts, the B-roll and the captions |
| **ElevenLabs** | Turns speech into text, and makes the sound effects |
| **Auphonic** | Cleans up the audio |
| **Cloudflare** | Stores the footage and the finished videos |
| **Blotato** | Posts the videos to Instagram, TikTok and YouTube |

## Free

| Service | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Supabase** | The login and where the scripts are saved |
| **Pexels** | Stock video clips |
| **Tavily** | Looks things up while writing a script |
| **GitHub** | Where the code is kept |

---

**If one runs out of credit:** the app says which one, and gives a link to top
it up. Most keep working on a backup — only **OpenRouter** and **Submagic**
stop things entirely.
